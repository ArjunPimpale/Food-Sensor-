ABOUT OUR PROJECT

ABOUT US

We are a dynamic group of twelve students from the Sardar Patel Institute of Technology, united by a shared commitment to addressing critical issues that affect our community. Our latest initiative is focused on a significant and pervasive problem: food adulteration. Food adulteration involves the addition or mixing of inferior, harmful, or prohibited substances in food, posing substantial health risks and undermining consumer trust.

OUR PROJECT

Our project, "Community Food Adulteration Checking," aims to empower individuals with the knowledge and tools needed to detect and prevent the consumption of adulterated food. By leveraging our collective expertise in electronics, programming, and data analysis, we are developing a robust and user-friendly system designed to identify common adulterants in everyday food items.

OUR GOAL

Through this initiative, we seek to promote public health, enhance food safety, and foster a more informed and vigilant community. We believe that by combining our technical skills and passion for social impact, we can contribute to a healthier and safer environment for all.
